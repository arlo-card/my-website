An Intro to Their Relationship:

Zealot and Grifter are a well known duo throughout the Wildstorm universe. It's unknown how long they've been traveling together but their initial meeting occured between the events of Team 7: Dead Reckoning and when they joined the WildC.A.Ts together. Their romantic relationship influences many of their interactions. As Cole is oftentimes the narrator for these comics, his feelings are much more explicit but Zealot is shown to harbor romantic feelings as well.
